Organisational Strategy & Transformation

I am an international executive and coach with a successful track record transforming multi-disciplined teams, moving them from a disorganised culture to high performing, through the use of pragmatic strategy and values, coaching, and the creation of lightweight tools and processes.

HOW I WORK

I take long term strategic views that enable a deep and sustained change for individuals, teams, leaders and organisations in general. 

At the heart of my work are principles and values. I use these to architect the correct tools, behaviours and coaching to create a high-performance culture. 

​

Then with a relentlessly pragmatical approach, make these changes real and understood, bringing about positive change through coaching, mentoring and working closely with leaders and teams.

Case Study - ITV
In a leadership role at ITV - the largest commercial broadcaster in the UK - I led the transformation of ITV Online to Agile. In combination with other changes, ITV Online moved from a £13million loss in 2012 to profit-making in 2012. The success of the ITV Online agile approach led other technology divisions to adopt and use agile with my and others support.

Steps included:

  • Creation of a multi-year strategy gaining buy-in from key stakeholders
  • Reducing toxic conflict between Product and Tech groups and slowly moving them to a new collaborative relationship to the point executive leadership merged the two departments as they were already working as one
  • Coaching of the online leadership team to align and work together on common objectives 
  • Creation of tools including portfolio board for delivery and a preparation board for the next quarters delivery along with quarterly planning tools
  • Initiation of a technology modernisation programme using iterative agile and Product techniques.
Case Study - Blacklane

Created an agile transformation department-wide strategy for a startup making $100M in revenue to move the product and technology department to a more efficient way of working

Steps included:

  • With the increasing size of the department, I led the reorganisation of the department and the creation of several new teams, along with creating a growth plan for the department. This included a holistic, prioritised recruitment view to scale the department further. 
  • I helped to hire and mentor my successor and worked with him to create a combined strategy that we worked on together. 
  • I created and implemented a successful strategy focused on improving delivery across all teams, including technical best practices and developer skills. 
  • Coached and created leaders from across the department
  • Successfully mentored the Director of Product on managing a team of 11 people.
  • I worked with stakeholders to hire a replacement Director of Product - aligning with key people to ensure the right fit for the company.
  • I acted as a knowledge expert for Product, teaching good practice and providing coaching to multiple product managers. 
  • I created a Cultural operating model for Engineering and Product based on values, principles and teal processes.
  • I was a supportive mentor and coach for people across the organisation, including developers and senior leadership. 
  • Created and coached a team of Agile coaches for the first time in the companies history to help mature the teams and department
