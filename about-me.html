My mission is to serve the world and change the world. I design and enable high-performance cultures. 

I am an international executive and coach with a successful track record transforming multi-disciplined teams, moving them from a disorganised culture to high performing agile through pragmatic strategy and values, agile coaching, and the creation of lightweight tools and processes.

 

Along with company-wide transformation, I have specialist knowledge in transforming product, engineering, agile and design teams. 

How I work

I take long term strategic views that enable a deep and sustained change for individuals, teams, leaders and organisations in general.

At the heart of my work are principles and values. I use these to architect the correct tools, behaviours and coaching to create an agile, high-performance culture. 

​

Then with a relentlessly pragmatical approach, make these changes real and understood, bringing about positive change through coaching, mentoring and working closely with teams.
